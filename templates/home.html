About Us

We strive to provide an experience of overall
well-being. We believe in providing service to the 3
main pillars of well-being : Comfort, Health and
Happiness. We do this by hosting our guests in our
world-class villas, providing top health foods from
our renowned health certified chef and by being able
to show the top surf spots of some of the world’s
most beautiful and conistent waves.

Comfort - Recover to go out harder

Our villas provide a sense of being around the local environment. We pride ourselves
in having the best villas available in all of the mentawai islands. Our villas will provide
the comfort needed after a long day of adventure and amazing waves.

Health is Wealth

Nothing is more important than ones own health and more and more
the world is waking up to that fact. Our world-renowned health certified chef has
loved calling the mentawai islands his home now for over ten years. Cooking with as
local ingredients as possible your body will thank you after each meal everyday.*
*3 meals/day

Ride the Wave of Your Dreams

The mentawai islands is surrounded by the most consistent reef anywhere in the
world. With the surf season being virtually all year long you will not get skunked
coming to surf in mentawai. The backdrops are beautiful along with amazing waves
we can almost gurantee you will have a smile from ear to ear.
